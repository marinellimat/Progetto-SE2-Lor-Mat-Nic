<p>Use Case:   Use Money Saving Option</p>

<p>Actors: 
• Registered User
• Car</p>

<p>Entry Condition:
• The registered user choose to use the Money Saving Option on the car display.</p>

<p>Flow of Events:
• The registered user inserts its destination.
• This information is sent to the system.
• The system determines an available charging station to ensure a uniform distribution of cars.
• The registered user receives the address of the selected station.</p>

<p>Exit Condition: 
• User recieves the address of the charging station</p>
